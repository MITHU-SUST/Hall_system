Key Features

📝 Easy Registration

Register with your 10-digit registration number, room number, and name. Quick and secure registration process.

⚡ Quick Complaint Filing

File complaints in multiple categories: Electricity, WiFi, Food, Maintenance, Security, Water, Noise, and more.

📊 Real-time Tracking

Track the status of your complaints in real-time. View pending, solving, and solved complaints instantly.

💬 Direct Communication

Receive updates and messages from hall authorities regarding your complaints with detailed responses.

👨‍💼 Admin Dashboard

Administrators can view, manage, and respond to all complaints from a centralized dashboard.

📈 Full History

Keep a complete record of all complaints and their resolution history for future reference.

About This System

The Syed Mujtoba Ali Hall Complaint Management System is designed to streamline the process of addressing student concerns and grievances. This system enables students to lodge complaints in various categories and allows hall authorities to efficiently manage and resolve these issues.

Our mission is to create a transparent, efficient, and responsive communication channel between students and hall administration, ensuring that all concerns are heard and addressed promptly.
